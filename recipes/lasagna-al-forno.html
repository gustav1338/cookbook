Lasagna al Forno

Ragù, béchamel, disciplined layers.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • Lasagna sheets
  • 700 ml ragù
  • 700 ml béchamel
  • 250 g mozzarella
  • 80 g Parmigiano-Reggiano
  • Butter

Instructions

  1. Butter the dish lightly.
  2. Layer béchamel, lasagna sheets, ragù, mozzarella, and Parmigiano; repeat.
  3. Finish with béchamel and Parmigiano.
  4. Bake at 190 C until bronzed and bubbling; rest before cutting.
