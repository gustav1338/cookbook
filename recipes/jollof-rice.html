Jollof Rice

Tomato-pepper base reduced before rice.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Tomatoes
  • Red bell peppers
  • Onion
  • Tomato paste
  • Stock
  • Thyme
  • Bay
  • Curry powder

Instructions

  1. Blend tomatoes, peppers, and onion.
  2. Fry tomato paste, then add the purée and reduce it well.
  3. Add stock and rice and cook gently until done.
  4. Steam briefly off heat before fluffing.
