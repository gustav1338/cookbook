Croquetas de Jamón

Velvety interior, fine crumb.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • 50 g butter
  • 50 g flour
  • 500 ml milk
  • 100 g jamón serrano, minced
  • Nutmeg
  • Eggs
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Oil for frying

Instructions

  1. Make a stiff béchamel and fold in jamón and nutmeg.
  2. Spread and chill until firm.
  3. Shape, bread, and chill again.
  4. Fry until crisp and deeply golden.
