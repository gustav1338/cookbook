Pozole Rojo

Hominy, chile broth, garnishes.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • Pork shoulder
  • Hominy
  • Dried guajillo chiles
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Oregano
  • Radish
  • Cabbage
  • Lime

Instructions

  1. Simmer pork until tender.
  2. Blend soaked chiles with onion and garlic, then fry the purée briefly.
  3. Combine with broth and hominy and simmer.
  4. Serve with radish, cabbage, oregano, and lime.
