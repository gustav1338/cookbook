Hainanese Chicken Rice

Poached chicken, seasoned rice, exact cuts.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • Whole chicken
  • Jasmine rice
  • Ginger
  • Garlic
  • Scallions
  • Soy sauce
  • Sesame oil
  • Cucumber
  • Chile sauce

Instructions

  1. Poach the chicken gently; chill briefly to set the skin if you like.
  2. Cook rice in chicken fat with ginger, garlic, and broth.
  3. Chop the chicken and serve with rice, cucumber, and sauces.
