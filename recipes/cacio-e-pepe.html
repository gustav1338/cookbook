Cacio e Pepe

Pasta, cheese, pepper, technique.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • 400 g tonnarelli or spaghetti
  • 160 g Pecorino Romano, finely grated
  • 2 tbsp cracked black pepper
  • Salt

Instructions

  1. Toast the pepper briefly in a dry pan.
  2. Boil pasta until just shy of done.
  3. Build a starchy emulsion with a little pasta water and the pepper.
  4. Toss in the pasta, then shower in Pecorino off the heat, adding water as needed until creamy.
