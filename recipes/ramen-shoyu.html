Ramen Shoyu

Clear soy broth, springy noodles.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • Stock
  • Soy tare
  • Ramen noodles
  • Chashu or chicken
  • Soft eggs
  • Nori
  • Scallions
  • Bamboo shoots

Instructions

  1. Season hot broth with tare.
  2. Cook noodles just to springy.
  3. Assemble with sliced meat, egg, nori, scallions, and bamboo shoots.
