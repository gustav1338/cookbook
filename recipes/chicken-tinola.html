Chicken Tinola

Ginger broth, greens, green papaya.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • Chicken pieces
  • Ginger
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Green papaya or chayote
  • Leafy greens
  • Fish sauce

Instructions

  1. Brown ginger lightly, then add garlic and onion.
  2. Add chicken and cook until opaque.
  3. Add water and simmer.
  4. Add papaya, then greens; season with fish sauce.
