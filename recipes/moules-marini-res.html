Moules Marinières

Mussels, wine, herbs, speed.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • 1.5 kg mussels
  • 2 shallots, minced
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 200 ml dry white wine
  • Parsley
  • Black pepper
  • Cream, optional

Instructions

  1. Scrub and debeard the mussels.
  2. Sweat shallots in butter; add wine and bring to a boil.
  3. Add mussels, cover, and steam until opened.
  4. Finish with parsley and a little cream if wanted.
