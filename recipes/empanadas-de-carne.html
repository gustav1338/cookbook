Empanadas de Carne

Buttery pastry, juicy filling.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • Empanada dough
  • Ground beef
  • Onion
  • Red pepper
  • Paprika
  • Cumin
  • Olives
  • Boiled eggs

Instructions

  1. Cook onion and pepper soft, then brown beef.
  2. Season well and fold in olives and chopped egg.
  3. Fill and crimp the dough.
  4. Bake until golden.
