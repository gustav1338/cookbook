Asado Chimichurri

Fire, salt, herbs.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • Steak or short ribs
  • Salt
  • Parsley
  • Garlic
  • Oregano
  • Red wine vinegar
  • Olive oil
  • Chile flakes

Instructions

  1. Salt the meat ahead if you have time.
  2. Grill over strong heat to your preferred doneness.
  3. Make chimichurri by chopping herbs with garlic, vinegar, oil, and chile.
  4. Rest the meat and spoon over the sauce.
