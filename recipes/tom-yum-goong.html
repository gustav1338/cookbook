Tom Yum Goong

Hot, sour, aromatic.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • 500 ml stock
  • Lemongrass
  • Galangal
  • Kaffir lime leaves
  • Mushrooms
  • Prawns
  • Fish sauce
  • Lime juice
  • Thai chiles
  • Cilantro

Instructions

  1. Bring stock to a simmer with lemongrass, galangal, and lime leaves.
  2. Add mushrooms and prawns; cook briefly.
  3. Season with fish sauce and lime juice.
  4. Finish with chiles and cilantro.
