Bibimbap

Rice, vegetables, egg, chile.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • Cooked rice
  • Beef or mushrooms
  • Spinach
  • Bean sprouts
  • Carrot
  • Zucchini
  • Eggs
  • Gochujang
  • Sesame oil

Instructions

  1. Cook each vegetable separately and season lightly.
  2. Brown the beef or mushrooms.
  3. Top hot rice with the arranged components and an egg.
  4. Dress with gochujang thinned with sesame oil and mix at the table.
