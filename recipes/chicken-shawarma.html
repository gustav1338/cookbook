Chicken Shawarma

Spiced, charred edges, sharp finish.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken thighs
  • Garlic
  • Lemon juice
  • Yogurt
  • Cumin
  • Coriander
  • Paprika
  • Cinnamon
  • Pita
  • Tahini sauce

Instructions

  1. Marinate the chicken with crushed garlic, lemon juice, yogurt, cumin, coriander, paprika, cinnamon, and salt.
  2. Roast hot or grill until bronzed and cooked through.
  3. Rest, slice, and toss with any accumulated juices.
  4. Serve in pita with tahini sauce and pickles.
