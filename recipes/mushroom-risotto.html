Mushroom Risotto

Earthy and restrained.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • 320 g Carnaroli rice
  • 300 g mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 shallot, minced
  • 1 L stock
  • 100 ml white wine
  • 50 g butter
  • 40 g Parmigiano
  • Parsley

Instructions

  1. Brown mushrooms hard; reserve some for finishing.
  2. Sweat shallot, toast rice, deglaze with wine.
  3. Add stock gradually until the rice is nearly done.
  4. Fold in mushrooms, butter, and Parmigiano; finish with parsley.
