Massaman Curry

Warm-spiced and slow.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • Beef chuck
  • Massaman curry paste
  • Coconut milk
  • Potatoes
  • Onion
  • Peanuts
  • Tamarind
  • Fish sauce
  • Palm sugar

Instructions

  1. Fry the curry paste in coconut cream.
  2. Add beef and braise gently in coconut milk until almost tender.
  3. Add potatoes, onion, and peanuts.
  4. Balance with tamarind, fish sauce, and sugar.
