Laksa

Coconut, chile, herbs, noodles.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • Laksa paste
  • Coconut milk
  • Stock
  • Rice noodles
  • Prawns or chicken
  • Tofu puffs
  • Bean sprouts
  • Cilantro

Instructions

  1. Fry the paste until aromatic.
  2. Add coconut milk and stock; simmer.
  3. Cook noodles and warm the protein in the broth.
  4. Serve with tofu puffs, sprouts, and herbs.
