Sinigang na Baboy

Tamarind-led pork soup.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • Pork ribs or shoulder
  • Tamarind
  • Tomatoes
  • Onion
  • Daikon
  • Green beans or okra
  • Eggplant
  • Fish sauce

Instructions

  1. Simmer the pork ribs or shoulder until nearly tender.
  2. Add the onion, tomatoes, daikon, and other tougher vegetables.
  3. Season the broth with tamarind and fish sauce.
  4. Add okra, eggplant, or other delicate vegetables at the end.
