Fried Chicken

Brined, dredged, rested, fried.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • Chicken pieces
  • Buttermilk
  • Hot sauce
  • Flour
  • Cornstarch
  • Paprika
  • Garlic powder
  • Cayenne
  • Oil

Instructions

  1. Marinate the chicken in buttermilk and hot sauce.
  2. Dredge heavily and rest until the crust hydrates.
  3. Fry at a steady moderate temperature until crisp and cooked through.
