Steak Frites

Hard sear, hot fries, sharp sauce optional.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • 2 ribeye or strip steaks
  • Potatoes for frying
  • Butter
  • Thyme
  • Garlic
  • Salt and black pepper

Instructions

  1. Cut and rinse the potatoes for fries; cook them once at low temperature, then again at high heat to crisp.
  2. Dry the steaks thoroughly and season with salt and black pepper.
  3. Sear hard, then baste with butter, thyme, and garlic.
  4. Rest the meat, salt the fries, and serve at once.
