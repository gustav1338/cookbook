Margherita Pizza

Tomato, mozzarella, basil, no clutter.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough, 2 balls
  • 300 g crushed tomatoes
  • 250 g fior di latte
  • Basil leaves
  • Olive oil
  • Salt

Instructions

  1. Stretch each pizza dough ball gently.
  2. Dress sparingly with the crushed tomatoes and salt.
  3. Add the fior di latte and bake on a stone or steel at maximum heat until blistered.
  4. Finish with basil leaves and olive oil.
