Dan Dan Noodles

Sesame, chile oil, pork.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • Wheat noodles
  • Ground pork
  • Ya cai or pickled mustard greens
  • Sesame paste
  • Chili oil
  • Light soy
  • Black vinegar
  • Scallions
  • Sichuan pepper

Instructions

  1. Brown pork with the preserved greens.
  2. Make a bowl sauce with sesame paste, soy, vinegar, chili oil, and a little noodle water.
  3. Cook noodles and toss in the sauce.
  4. Top with pork, scallions, and Sichuan pepper.
