Amatriciana

Tomato, guanciale, chile, Pecorino.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • 400 g bucatini
  • 150 g guanciale, batons
  • 400 g canned tomatoes, crushed
  • 1 small dried chile
  • 60 g Pecorino Romano
  • Salt

Instructions

  1. Render guanciale until lightly crisp; bloom the chile in the fat.
  2. Add tomatoes and simmer until slightly concentrated.
  3. Boil pasta to al dente and finish in the sauce.
  4. Serve with ample Pecorino.
