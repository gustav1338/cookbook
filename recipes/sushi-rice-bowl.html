Sushi Rice Bowl

A bowl version of sushi logic.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • Japanese rice
  • Rice vinegar
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Raw fish or cooked salmon
  • Cucumber
  • Avocado
  • Nori
  • Soy sauce
  • Wasabi

Instructions

  1. Cook the rice and season while warm with vinegar, sugar, and salt.
  2. Arrange fish, vegetables, and nori over the rice.
  3. Serve with soy and wasabi.
