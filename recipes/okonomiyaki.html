Okonomiyaki

Savory pancake, loose center, crisp edges.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • Cabbage, shredded
  • Flour
  • Dashi
  • Eggs
  • Pork belly or shrimp
  • Okonomiyaki sauce
  • Kewpie mayonnaise
  • Bonito flakes

Instructions

  1. Mix flour, dashi, eggs, and cabbage into a loose batter.
  2. Cook in a hot pan with pork or shrimp until set on both sides.
  3. Dress with sauce, mayonnaise, and bonito.
