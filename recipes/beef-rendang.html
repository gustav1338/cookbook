Beef Rendang

Dry curry by reduction, not haste.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • Beef chuck
  • Coconut milk
  • Lemongrass
  • Galangal
  • Garlic
  • Shallots
  • Chiles
  • Turmeric
  • Kaffir lime leaves

Instructions

  1. Blend the aromatics into a paste and fry briefly.
  2. Add beef and coconut milk.
  3. Simmer slowly until the liquid reduces, the oil separates, and the beef is dark and tender.
