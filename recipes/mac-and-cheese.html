Mac and Cheese

Sharp sauce, proper crust.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • Elbow macaroni
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Cheddar
  • Gruyère or Parmesan
  • Mustard powder
  • Breadcrumbs

Instructions

  1. Cook macaroni short of finished.
  2. Make a béchamel and melt in the cheeses.
  3. Combine with pasta.
  4. Top with crumbs and bake until bubbling and bronzed.
