Lomo Saltado

Wok heat meets soy and vinegar.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • Beef sirloin
  • Red onion
  • Tomatoes
  • Soy sauce
  • Vinegar
  • Garlic
  • Cilantro
  • Fries
  • Rice

Instructions

  1. Sear the beef hard in batches.
  2. Wok-fry onion and tomatoes briefly.
  3. Return beef with soy, vinegar, and garlic; toss fast.
  4. Finish with cilantro and serve with fries and rice.
