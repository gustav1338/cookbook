Pierogi Ruskie

Potato, cheese, onion.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • Dough for dumplings
  • Potatoes
  • Farmer cheese or ricotta
  • Onion
  • Butter
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Boil and mash potatoes dry.
  2. Mix with cheese, onion, salt, and pepper.
  3. Fill the dough, boil until they float, then pan-fry in butter if desired.
