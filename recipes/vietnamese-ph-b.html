Vietnamese Phở Bò

Clear broth, precise garnishes.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • Beef bones
  • Onion
  • Ginger
  • Star anise
  • Cinnamon
  • Fish sauce
  • Rice noodles
  • Thin beef slices
  • Scallions
  • Herbs

Instructions

  1. Char onion and ginger.
  2. Simmer bones with aromatics until the broth is clear and deep.
  3. Season with fish sauce.
  4. Cook noodles, top with raw beef, and ladle over boiling broth.
  5. Serve with herbs and lime.
