Dal Tadka

Lentils with a final spice bloom.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • Yellow lentils
  • Turmeric
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Ghee
  • Cumin seeds
  • Mustard seeds
  • Dried red chiles
  • Tomato
  • Cilantro

Instructions

  1. Simmer lentils with turmeric until soft.
  2. Cook tomato, garlic, and ginger into the dal.
  3. Bloom cumin, mustard, and chiles in hot ghee and pour over.
  4. Finish with cilantro.
