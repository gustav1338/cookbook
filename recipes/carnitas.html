Carnitas

Confit-then-crisp pork.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • Pork shoulder
  • Orange
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Bay
  • Lard or neutral oil
  • Salt

Instructions

  1. Braise or confit the pork slowly with orange, onion, garlic, and bay until tender.
  2. Pull into chunks and crisp in hot fat or a hot oven.
  3. Serve with tortillas and salsa.
