Birria Tacos

Rich braise with chile consommé.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • Beef chuck or goat
  • Dried guajillo and ancho chiles
  • Tomatoes
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Cloves
  • Cinnamon
  • Tortillas
  • Cheese, optional

Instructions

  1. Blend the chile base and marinate the meat.
  2. Braise until shreddable.
  3. Dip tortillas in the fat and griddle with meat, and cheese if using.
  4. Serve with the strained consommé.
