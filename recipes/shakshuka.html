Shakshuka

Eggs set in spiced tomato-pepper stew.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • Olive oil
  • 1 onion
  • 1 red pepper
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 400 g tomatoes
  • 4 eggs
  • Parsley

Instructions

  1. Cook onion and pepper soft in olive oil.
  2. Add garlic and spices, then tomatoes; simmer until thick.
  3. Make wells and add eggs.
  4. Cover and cook until the whites set; finish with parsley.
