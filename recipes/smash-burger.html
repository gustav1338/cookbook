Smash Burger

Hard smash, soft bun.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • Ground beef, loosely portioned
  • American cheese
  • Soft buns
  • Onion, optional
  • Pickles
  • Mustard
  • Ketchup

Instructions

  1. Heat a steel or cast-iron surface until very hot.
  2. Smash the beef thin and salt it.
  3. Flip when deeply crusted, top with cheese, and stack on toasted buns.
