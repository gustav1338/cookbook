Chana Masala

Chickpeas in a dryish, tart gravy.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • Cooked chickpeas
  • Onion
  • Tomatoes
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Cumin
  • Coriander
  • Turmeric
  • Garam masala
  • Amchur or lemon

Instructions

  1. Brown onion well.
  2. Add garlic, ginger, and spices, then tomatoes.
  3. Cook down until the masala is thick.
  4. Add chickpeas and a little water; simmer.
  5. Finish with amchur or lemon.
