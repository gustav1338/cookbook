Spring Rolls

Fresh herbs, tight wrap, clean dip.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • Rice paper
  • Cooked shrimp or pork
  • Rice vermicelli
  • Lettuce
  • Mint
  • Cilantro
  • Thai basil
  • Peanut or hoisin dip

Instructions

  1. Soften each rice paper briefly.
  2. Layer lettuce, noodles, herbs, and protein.
  3. Roll tightly, keeping the cylinder neat.
  4. Serve with dip.
