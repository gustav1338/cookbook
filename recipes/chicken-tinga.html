Chicken Tinga

Chipotle, tomato, onion.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • Cooked chicken
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomatoes
  • Chipotle in adobo
  • Oregano

Instructions

  1. Cook onion and garlic soft.
  2. Blend tomatoes with chipotle, then simmer to thicken.
  3. Fold in shredded chicken and oregano.
