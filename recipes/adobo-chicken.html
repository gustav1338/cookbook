Adobo Chicken

Vinegar first, then patience.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • Chicken pieces
  • Soy sauce
  • Vinegar
  • Garlic
  • Bay leaves
  • Black peppercorns
  • Sugar, optional

Instructions

  1. Brown the chicken lightly.
  2. Add garlic, bay, peppercorns, soy, and vinegar; boil briefly without stirring.
  3. Reduce to a glossy, sharp-salty glaze.
