Butter Noodles with Chili Crisp

Not classical, but current and deserved.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • Wheat noodles
  • Butter
  • Garlic
  • Soy sauce
  • Chili crisp
  • Scallions

Instructions

  1. Cook noodles until springy.
  2. Brown butter lightly with garlic.
  3. Toss noodles with soy and chili crisp.
  4. Finish with scallions.
