Bobotie

Curried mince with custard top.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • Ground beef or lamb
  • Onion
  • Curry powder
  • Apricot jam or chutney
  • Bread
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Raisins

Instructions

  1. Cook onion and mince until browned.
  2. Season with curry and chutney; fold in soaked bread and raisins.
  3. Bake topped with an egg-milk custard until set and browned.
