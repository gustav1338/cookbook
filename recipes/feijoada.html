Feijoada

Beans, pork, orange, farofa.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • Black beans
  • Mixed pork cuts and sausage
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Bay
  • Orange
  • Rice
  • Collards

Instructions

  1. Soak and simmer beans until nearly tender.
  2. Brown the meats separately.
  3. Cook onion and garlic, then combine everything with bay and simmer until rich.
  4. Serve with rice, collards, orange, and farofa.
