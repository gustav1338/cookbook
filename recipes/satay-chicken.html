Satay Chicken

Charred skewers, peanut sauce.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • Chicken thigh
  • Garlic
  • Coriander
  • Turmeric
  • Soy sauce
  • Palm sugar
  • Skewers
  • Peanut sauce

Instructions

  1. Marinate chicken with spices and sugar.
  2. Skewer and grill until charred.
  3. Serve with peanut sauce.
