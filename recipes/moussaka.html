Moussaka

Eggplant, meat sauce, béchamel.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • Eggplants
  • 500 g lamb or beef mince
  • 1 onion
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 400 g tomatoes
  • Cinnamon
  • Red wine
  • Béchamel
  • Parmesan or kefalotyri

Instructions

  1. Brown the eggplant slices in olive oil or roast them.
  2. Cook onion, garlic, and meat until well colored; add tomatoes, cinnamon, and wine and reduce.
  3. Layer eggplant and meat sauce.
  4. Top with béchamel and cheese; bake until deeply bronzed.
