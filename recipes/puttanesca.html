Puttanesca

Briny, fast, pantry-driven.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • 400 g spaghetti
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 4 anchovy fillets
  • 3 garlic cloves, sliced
  • 400 g canned tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp capers
  • 12 olives, torn
  • 1 tsp chile flakes
  • Parsley

Instructions

  1. Warm the olive oil; melt the anchovy fillets into it.
  2. Add the sliced garlic and chile flakes; cook briefly.
  3. Add the crushed tomatoes, capers, and torn olives; simmer 10 minutes.
  4. Boil the spaghetti, finish it in the sauce, and fold through parsley.
