Japchae

Glass noodles, sesame, balance.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • Sweet potato noodles
  • Beef or mushrooms
  • Spinach
  • Carrot
  • Onion
  • Soy sauce
  • Sugar
  • Sesame oil

Instructions

  1. Cook noodles until bouncy and toss with sesame oil.
  2. Cook vegetables and meat separately.
  3. Combine with soy and sugar to taste.
