Naan

Tender crumb, charred blisters.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Yogurt
  • Milk or water
  • Yeast
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Ghee

Instructions

  1. Mix and knead a soft dough; let it rise.
  2. Portion and stretch into teardrops.
  3. Cook on a very hot steel or cast iron, finishing with a live flame if possible.
  4. Brush with ghee.
