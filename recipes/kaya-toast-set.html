Kaya Toast Set

Toast, coconut jam, soft eggs.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • White bread
  • Kaya
  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Soy sauce
  • White pepper

Instructions

  1. Toast the bread hard and fill with kaya and cold butter.
  2. Cook the eggs to soft-set.
  3. Serve with soy and white pepper.
