Coq au Vin

Wine-braised chicken with proper reduction.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken, jointed
  • 150 g bacon lardons
  • 12 pearl onions
  • 250 g mushrooms
  • 500 ml red wine
  • 250 ml stock
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • Thyme
  • Bay
  • Butter

Instructions

  1. Brown the bacon lardons; remove. Brown the chicken in the fat and season.
  2. Add the pearl onions and mushrooms; color lightly.
  3. Dust with flour, then add the red wine, stock, thyme, and bay.
  4. Braise gently until tender. Reduce the sauce separately if needed, then return everything to gloss.
