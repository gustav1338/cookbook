Green Curry Chicken

Fresh paste, coconut, herbs.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • Chicken thigh
  • Green curry paste
  • Coconut milk
  • Thai eggplants or zucchini
  • Fish sauce
  • Palm sugar
  • Thai basil
  • Makrut lime leaves

Instructions

  1. Crack a little coconut milk in a pan and fry the green curry paste until fragrant.
  2. Add the chicken thigh and coat it well in the paste.
  3. Pour in the remaining coconut milk and simmer.
  4. Add the eggplants or zucchini, then season with fish sauce and palm sugar.
  5. Finish with Thai basil and makrut lime leaves.
