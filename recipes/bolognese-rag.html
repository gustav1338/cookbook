Bolognese Ragù

Slow, reduced, dairy-softened.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • 500 g ground beef and pork mix
  • 1 onion, finely diced
  • 1 carrot, finely diced
  • 1 celery stalk, finely diced
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 180 ml dry white wine
  • 500 ml milk
  • 400 ml stock
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and black pepper

Instructions

  1. Sweat the soffritto in olive oil until sweet.
  2. Brown the meat thoroughly; season.
  3. Work in tomato paste, then deglaze with wine.
  4. Add milk and reduce until nearly dry. Add stock and simmer gently until dense and brick-red.
  5. Toss with tagliatelle; finish with Parmigiano.
