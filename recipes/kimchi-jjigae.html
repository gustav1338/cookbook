Kimchi Jjigae

Sour kimchi drives the stew.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • Kimchi
  • Pork belly or tuna
  • Tofu
  • Gochugaru
  • Garlic
  • Scallions
  • Stock

Instructions

  1. Cook kimchi with pork until the fat and chile stain the pot.
  2. Add stock and simmer.
  3. Add tofu and finish with scallions.
