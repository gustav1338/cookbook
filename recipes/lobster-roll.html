Lobster Roll

Cold and spare, or warm with butter.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • Cooked lobster meat
  • Mayonnaise or butter
  • Lemon
  • Chives
  • Split-top buns

Instructions

  1. Dress the lobster meat lightly with mayonnaise, lemon, and chives, or warm it in butter.
  2. Toast the split-top buns in butter.
  3. Fill generously and serve at once.
