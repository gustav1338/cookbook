Niçoise Salad

Composed, not tossed to death.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • Tuna, seared or oil-packed
  • Small potatoes
  • Green beans
  • Tomatoes
  • Hard-boiled eggs
  • Olives
  • Anchovies
  • Lettuce
  • Olive oil
  • Red wine vinegar
  • Dijon mustard

Instructions

  1. Boil the potatoes and green beans separately until just tender.
  2. Whisk a sharp vinaigrette with olive oil, red wine vinegar, and Dijon.
  3. Arrange lettuce, potatoes, beans, tomatoes, eggs, tuna, olives, and anchovies.
  4. Dress lightly at the table.
