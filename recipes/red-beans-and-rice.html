Red Beans and Rice

Smoked pork, beans, time.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • Red beans
  • Andouille sausage
  • Onion
  • Celery
  • Bell pepper
  • Garlic
  • Bay
  • Thyme
  • Rice

Instructions

  1. Soak and simmer beans until softening.
  2. Brown sausage; cook onion, celery, pepper, and garlic.
  3. Combine and simmer until creamy, mashing a few beans into the pot.
  4. Serve over rice.
