Butter Chicken

Tomato, cream, fenugreek, smoke optional.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • Chicken thigh
  • Yogurt
  • Garam masala
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Butter
  • Tomatoes
  • Cream
  • Kasoori methi
  • Chile powder

Instructions

  1. Marinate the chicken in yogurt, garam masala, ginger-garlic paste, chile powder, and salt; roast or sear until colored.
  2. Cook the tomatoes in butter with any remaining ginger-garlic paste until sweet; blend if you want a smoother sauce.
  3. Simmer with cream and crushed kasoori methi.
  4. Add the chicken and cook gently until the sauce clings.
