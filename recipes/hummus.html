Hummus

Smooth, warm, properly seasoned.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • 300 g cooked chickpeas
  • 80 g tahini
  • 1 garlic clove
  • Lemon juice
  • Ice water
  • Olive oil
  • Cumin
  • Salt

Instructions

  1. Blend chickpeas, tahini, garlic, lemon, and salt until smooth.
  2. Add ice water gradually until the texture turns light and creamy.
  3. Plate with olive oil and a little cumin.
