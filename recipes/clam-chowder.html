Clam Chowder

Creamy but not gluey.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • Clams
  • Bacon
  • Onion
  • Celery
  • Potatoes
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Milk or cream
  • Thyme

Instructions

  1. Cook bacon and reserve.
  2. Sweat onion and celery; stir in flour.
  3. Add clam liquor and potatoes and simmer until tender.
  4. Add milk or cream and clams just to heat through.
