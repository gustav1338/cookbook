Chicken Katsu Curry

Cutlet plus mild curry, no argument.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • Chicken cutlets
  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Panko
  • Japanese curry roux
  • Onion
  • Carrot
  • Potato
  • Rice

Instructions

  1. Bread the chicken and fry until crisp.
  2. Cook onion, carrot, and potato until soft.
  3. Add water and curry roux; simmer until smooth.
  4. Slice the cutlet and serve over rice with curry.
