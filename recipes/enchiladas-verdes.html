Enchiladas Verdes

Tortillas dipped and rolled, not drowned.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • Corn tortillas
  • Chicken
  • Tomatillos
  • Serranos
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Cilantro
  • Crema
  • Cheese

Instructions

  1. Boil or roast tomatillos and chiles, then blend with onion, garlic, and cilantro.
  2. Lightly fry or warm tortillas.
  3. Fill with chicken, roll, and cover with salsa.
  4. Warm through and finish with crema and cheese.
