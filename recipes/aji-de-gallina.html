Aji de Gallina

Shredded chicken in a creamy chile sauce.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • Cooked chicken
  • Bread
  • Milk
  • Aji amarillo
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Walnuts
  • Parmesan

Instructions

  1. Cook onion and garlic soft.
  2. Blend bread, milk, chile, and walnuts.
  3. Return to the pan and simmer gently, then fold in chicken and Parmesan.
