Spanakopita

Spinach and feta held in crisp phyllo.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • Phyllo pastry
  • 500 g spinach
  • 200 g feta
  • 1 onion
  • 2 eggs
  • Dill
  • Butter or olive oil

Instructions

  1. Cook the onion soft and wilt the spinach dry.
  2. Mix the spinach with feta, eggs, and dill.
  3. Layer the phyllo with butter or olive oil, add the filling, and cover with more phyllo.
  4. Score and bake until crisp.
