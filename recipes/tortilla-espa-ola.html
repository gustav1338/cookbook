Tortilla Española

Potato, onion, egg, patience.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • 700 g waxy potatoes
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 6 eggs
  • Olive oil
  • Salt

Instructions

  1. Poach the potatoes and onion gently in olive oil until tender, not crisp.
  2. Drain them well and cool slightly.
  3. Mix with the beaten eggs and salt.
  4. Cook over moderate heat, flip, and finish until just set inside.
