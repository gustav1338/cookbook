Shrimp and Grits

Sharp grits, quick shrimp.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • Shrimp
  • Stone-ground grits
  • Stock or water
  • Cheddar
  • Butter
  • Bacon
  • Scallions
  • Lemon

Instructions

  1. Cook grits slowly until fully tender.
  2. Finish with butter and cheddar.
  3. Cook bacon, then sear shrimp in the rendered fat.
  4. Serve shrimp over grits with scallions and lemon.
