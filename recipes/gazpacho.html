Gazpacho

Cold, smooth, direct.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Cucumber
  • Green pepper
  • Garlic
  • Stale bread
  • Sherry vinegar
  • Olive oil
  • Salt

Instructions

  1. Soak the bread briefly.
  2. Blend everything until completely smooth.
  3. Adjust with water, vinegar, and salt.
  4. Chill thoroughly before serving.
