Patatas Bravas

Crisp potatoes, assertive sauce.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Olive oil
  • 1 onion
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 tbsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp hot paprika
  • 400 g tomatoes
  • Sherry vinegar
  • Aioli, optional

Instructions

  1. Roast or fry potatoes until crisp.
  2. Cook onion and garlic soft; add paprikas and tomatoes.
  3. Simmer and blend the sauce until smooth.
  4. Dress potatoes with bravas sauce and aioli if using.
