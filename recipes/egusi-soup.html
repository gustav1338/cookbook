Egusi Soup

Ground melon seed stew with greens.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • Egusi
  • Palm oil
  • Spinach or bitter greens
  • Stock
  • Onion
  • Scotch bonnet
  • Dried fish or meat

Instructions

  1. Fry onion in palm oil.
  2. Add egusi and cook until the raw taste leaves.
  3. Add stock and protein and simmer.
  4. Finish with greens and chile.
