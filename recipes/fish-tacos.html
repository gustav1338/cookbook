Fish Tacos

Cabbage, lime, chile, crunch.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • White fish
  • Cumin
  • Paprika
  • Lime
  • Corn tortillas
  • Cabbage
  • Crema or mayo
  • Chile sauce

Instructions

  1. Season and sear the fish until just cooked.
  2. Warm the tortillas.
  3. Serve with cabbage, crema, lime, and chile sauce.
