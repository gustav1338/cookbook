Nasi Goreng

Dark, fried, aromatic.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • Cooked rice, day-old
  • Chicken or shrimp
  • Shallots
  • Garlic
  • Sambal oelek
  • Kecap manis
  • Eggs
  • Scallions

Instructions

  1. Fry shallot and garlic until fragrant.
  2. Add protein, then rice, breaking it up well.
  3. Season with sambal and kecap manis until dark and smoky.
  4. Top with a fried egg.
