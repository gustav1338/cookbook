Moqueca

Fish stew with coconut and dendê.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • Firm fish
  • Onion
  • Tomatoes
  • Bell pepper
  • Coconut milk
  • Dendê oil
  • Cilantro
  • Lime

Instructions

  1. Layer onion, tomatoes, pepper, and fish in a pot.
  2. Season and add coconut milk and a little water.
  3. Simmer gently until the fish is just done.
  4. Finish with dendê, cilantro, and lime.
