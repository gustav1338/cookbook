Ceviche

Acid, fish, onion, heat.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • Very fresh white fish
  • Lime juice
  • Red onion
  • Aji amarillo or chile
  • Cilantro
  • Salt
  • Sweet potato, optional

Instructions

  1. Dice the fish and salt it lightly.
  2. Dress with lime, onion, chile, and cilantro.
  3. Serve immediately, before the acid fully cures the flesh.
