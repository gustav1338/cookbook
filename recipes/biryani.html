Biryani

Layered rice and meat; dum if possible.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • Basmati rice
  • Chicken or lamb
  • Yogurt
  • Onion
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Biryani masala
  • Mint
  • Cilantro
  • Saffron
  • Ghee

Instructions

  1. Marinate the chicken or lamb in yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, biryani masala, and salt.
  2. Brown the onion in ghee; cook the marinated meat until the masala is thick.
  3. Parboil the basmati rice.
  4. Layer rice and meat with the fried onion, mint, cilantro, saffron, and more ghee.
  5. Seal and steam gently until the grains finish and the layers merge.
