Palak Paneer

Spinach, paneer, green heat.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • Spinach
  • Paneer
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Green chiles
  • Cumin
  • Cream or yogurt

Instructions

  1. Blanch and purée the spinach.
  2. Brown paneer lightly.
  3. Cook onion, garlic, ginger, and chile; add spinach and simmer briefly.
  4. Fold in paneer and finish with a little cream or yogurt.
