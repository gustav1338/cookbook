Ratatouille

Vegetables cooked separately, then joined.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • 1 eggplant
  • 2 zucchini
  • 1 red pepper
  • 1 yellow pepper
  • 1 onion
  • 4 tomatoes
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • Olive oil
  • Thyme

Instructions

  1. Salt and drain the eggplant briefly if needed.
  2. Cook each vegetable in olive oil until just tender and distinct.
  3. Stew onion, garlic, tomatoes, and thyme to a loose base.
  4. Fold everything together and simmer briefly.
