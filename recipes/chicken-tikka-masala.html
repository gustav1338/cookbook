Chicken Tikka Masala

Tandoori-style pieces in a spiced tomato gravy.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • Chicken thigh
  • Yogurt
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Cumin
  • Coriander
  • Tomatoes
  • Cream
  • Onion
  • Garam masala

Instructions

  1. Marinate the chicken in yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, cumin, coriander, and a little salt; char or roast until marked.
  2. Cook the onion until golden, then add any remaining ginger-garlic paste, garam masala, and the tomatoes.
  3. Reduce the sauce, enrich with cream, then fold in the chicken and finish over low heat.
