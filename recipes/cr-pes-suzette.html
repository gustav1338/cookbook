Crêpes Suzette

Thin crêpes, orange, caramel, flame if you like.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • 125 g flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 300 ml milk
  • 30 g melted butter
  • Orange zest
  • Orange juice
  • Sugar
  • Butter
  • Grand Marnier

Instructions

  1. Make a smooth batter and rest it.
  2. Cook thin crêpes in a lightly buttered pan.
  3. Caramelize sugar lightly with butter, orange zest, and juice.
  4. Fold the crêpes into the sauce; add liqueur and flame if desired.
