Pad Thai

Tamarind-led, balanced, quick.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • 200 g rice noodles
  • 200 g shrimp or tofu
  • 2 eggs
  • Bean sprouts
  • Garlic chives
  • Tamarind
  • Fish sauce
  • Palm sugar
  • Roasted peanuts

Instructions

  1. Soak the rice noodles until pliable.
  2. Make a sauce with tamarind, fish sauce, and palm sugar.
  3. Stir-fry the shrimp or tofu, then add the noodles and sauce.
  4. Push everything aside, scramble the eggs, then fold through the bean sprouts and garlic chives.
  5. Finish with roasted peanuts and lime if you have it.
