Bún Chả

Charred pork, herbs, dipping broth.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • Pork shoulder, sliced
  • Pork mince
  • Fish sauce
  • Sugar
  • Garlic
  • Shallot
  • Rice vermicelli
  • Herbs
  • Pickled carrot and daikon

Instructions

  1. Marinate sliced and minced pork with fish sauce, sugar, garlic, and shallot.
  2. Grill until charred.
  3. Make a light sweet-sour fish sauce broth.
  4. Serve pork, noodles, herbs, and pickles separately.
