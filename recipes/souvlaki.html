Souvlaki

Charred skewers, lemon, oregano.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • 600 g pork shoulder or chicken thigh
  • Garlic
  • Lemon juice
  • Olive oil
  • Dried oregano
  • Yogurt
  • Pita
  • Red onion

Instructions

  1. Marinate the meat with garlic, lemon, oil, and oregano.
  2. Skewer and grill hard until charred and cooked through.
  3. Serve with pita, onion, and yogurt or tzatziki.
