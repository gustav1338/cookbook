Mole Poblano

Dark sauce, layered bitterness.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Dried chiles
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomatoes
  • Sesame
  • Almonds or peanuts
  • Raisins
  • Spices
  • Chocolate

Instructions

  1. Toast the chiles, nuts, seeds, and aromatics separately.
  2. Blend with tomatoes to a smooth paste.
  3. Fry the paste slowly until darker and fragrant, then thin with stock.
  4. Finish with a little chocolate and serve over chicken.
