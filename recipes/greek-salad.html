Greek Salad

Tomato-led, no lettuce by default.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Cucumber
  • Green pepper
  • Red onion
  • Kalamata olives
  • Feta
  • Olive oil
  • Dried oregano
  • Red wine vinegar

Instructions

  1. Cut vegetables into generous pieces.
  2. Season and dress with oil and a little vinegar.
  3. Top with feta and oregano.
