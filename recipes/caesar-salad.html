Caesar Salad

Anchovy, garlic, yolk, restraint.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • Romaine
  • Anchovies
  • Garlic
  • Egg yolk or mayo
  • Lemon
  • Parmesan
  • Olive oil
  • Croutons
  • Black pepper

Instructions

  1. Pound anchovy and garlic to a paste.
  2. Whisk with yolk, lemon, and oil into a sharp dressing.
  3. Dress romaine lightly and finish with Parmesan, croutons, and pepper.
