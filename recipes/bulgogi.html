Bulgogi

Sweet-salty beef with heat underneath.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • 500 g thinly sliced beef
  • Soy sauce
  • Sugar or pear purée
  • Garlic
  • Sesame oil
  • Scallions
  • Black pepper

Instructions

  1. Marinate the beef briefly.
  2. Sear in a very hot pan without crowding.
  3. Reduce the juices to a lacquer and fold back through the meat.
