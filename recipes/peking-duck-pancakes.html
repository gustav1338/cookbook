Peking Duck Pancakes

Crisp skin, sweet-salty trim.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • Duck legs or breasts, or bought roast duck
  • Mandarin pancakes
  • Scallions
  • Cucumber
  • Hoisin sauce

Instructions

  1. Roast or reheat duck until the skin is crisp.
  2. Warm the pancakes.
  3. Slice duck finely.
  4. Serve with scallions, cucumber, and hoisin.
