Couscous Royale

Steamed grains, rich broth, mixed meats.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • Couscous
  • Lamb pieces
  • Chicken pieces
  • Merguez
  • Carrots
  • Turnips
  • Chickpeas
  • Tomatoes
  • Harissa

Instructions

  1. Build a broth with the lamb, chicken, onion, tomatoes, and spices.
  2. Cook the carrots, turnips, and chickpeas in the broth until tender.
  3. Steam the couscous, fluff it, and moisten with broth.
  4. Serve with the meats, vegetables, merguez, and harissa.
