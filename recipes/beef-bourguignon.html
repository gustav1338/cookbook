Beef Bourguignon

A stew built on searing and reduction.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • 1 kg beef chuck, large cubes
  • 150 g bacon lardons
  • 2 carrots
  • 1 onion
  • 500 ml red wine
  • 500 ml stock
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • 12 pearl onions
  • 250 g mushrooms
  • Thyme
  • Bay

Instructions

  1. Brown the bacon lardons, then sear the beef in batches until dark.
  2. Cook the onion and carrots in the same pot.
  3. Return the beef, dust with flour, then add the red wine, stock, thyme, and bay.
  4. Braise until spoon-tender; finish with the pearl onions and sautéed mushrooms.
