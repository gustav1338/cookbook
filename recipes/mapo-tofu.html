Mapo Tofu

Bean paste, peppercorn, silk.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • 400 g soft tofu
  • 150 g ground pork
  • 2 tbsp doubanjiang
  • 1 tbsp fermented black beans
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Scallions
  • Sichuan peppercorn
  • Stock
  • Cornstarch

Instructions

  1. Brown the pork well.
  2. Add doubanjiang, black beans, garlic, and ginger; fry until fragrant.
  3. Add stock and tofu; simmer gently.
  4. Thicken lightly and finish with scallions and ground Sichuan pepper.
