Chicken Tagine with Preserved Lemon

Savory, bright, lightly perfumed.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken pieces
  • 2 onions, sliced
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 preserved lemon
  • Green olives
  • Ginger
  • Turmeric
  • Cinnamon stick
  • Cilantro
  • Parsley

Instructions

  1. Brown the chicken lightly.
  2. Cook onions and garlic until soft; add spices.
  3. Return chicken with a little water; braise gently.
  4. Finish with preserved lemon, olives, cilantro, and parsley.
