Suya

Spiced beef skewers, dry heat.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • Beef strips
  • Ground peanuts
  • Paprika
  • Cayenne
  • Ginger
  • Garlic powder
  • Onion powder
  • Oil
  • Salt

Instructions

  1. Coat beef lightly in oil, then in the suya spice mix.
  2. Skewer and grill over high heat.
  3. Finish with more spice and sliced onion.
