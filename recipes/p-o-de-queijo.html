Pão de Queijo

Cheese rolls with a springy crumb.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • Tapioca starch
  • Milk
  • Oil
  • Eggs
  • Minas cheese or Parmesan

Instructions

  1. Heat milk and oil to just boiling.
  2. Mix into tapioca starch, then cool slightly.
  3. Beat in eggs and cheese.
  4. Bake until puffed and lightly golden.
