Risotto alla Milanese

Saffron, stock, patience.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • 320 g Carnaroli rice
  • 1 small onion, minced
  • 1 L hot stock
  • 100 ml dry white wine
  • 1 pinch saffron
  • 50 g butter
  • 50 g Parmigiano-Reggiano

Instructions

  1. Sweat onion in butter without color.
  2. Toast the rice, then add wine and reduce.
  3. Add hot stock gradually, stirring often. Infuse saffron in stock and add midway.
  4. When the rice is loose and al dente, beat in butter and Parmigiano.
