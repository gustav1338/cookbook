Falafel

Use soaked chickpeas, never canned.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • 250 g dried chickpeas, soaked
  • 1 onion
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • Parsley
  • Cilantro
  • Cumin
  • Coriander
  • Baking powder
  • Oil for frying

Instructions

  1. Pulse everything to a coarse green paste.
  2. Rest the mixture briefly, then shape.
  3. Fry until dark golden and fully crisp.
  4. Serve with tahini, salad, and pita.
