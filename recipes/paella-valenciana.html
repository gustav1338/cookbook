Paella Valenciana

Rice, saffron, socarrat.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • 400 g paella rice
  • Chicken pieces
  • Rabbit pieces or extra chicken
  • Green beans
  • Tomatoes, grated
  • 1 pinch saffron
  • 1.2 L stock or water
  • Olive oil
  • Paprika

Instructions

  1. Brown the chicken and rabbit well.
  2. Cook the green beans, then add the grated tomatoes and paprika.
  3. Add the stock or water and saffron; season.
  4. Rain in the paella rice evenly and do not stir again.
  5. Cook until the liquid is absorbed and a light socarrat forms.
