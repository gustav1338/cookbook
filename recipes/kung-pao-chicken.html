Kung Pao Chicken

Dry chiles, peanuts, quick lacquer.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • Chicken thigh
  • Dry chiles
  • Sichuan peppercorn
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Scallions
  • Peanuts
  • Light soy
  • Dark soy
  • Chinkiang vinegar
  • Sugar
  • Cornstarch

Instructions

  1. Velvet the chicken lightly with starch.
  2. Fry chiles and peppercorns briefly.
  3. Stir-fry chicken hard, then add garlic, ginger, and scallions.
  4. Add the sauce and reduce to a glossy coat.
  5. Finish with peanuts.
