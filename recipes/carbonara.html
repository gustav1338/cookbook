Carbonara

Silky, peppery, no cream.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • 400 g spaghetti
  • 150 g guanciale, diced
  • 3 egg yolks + 1 whole egg
  • 80 g Pecorino Romano, finely grated
  • 20 g Parmigiano-Reggiano, finely grated
  • 2 tsp cracked black pepper
  • Salt

Instructions

  1. Render the guanciale slowly until crisp at the edges; keep the fat in the pan.
  2. Boil the spaghetti in lightly salted water until just shy of al dente.
  3. Whisk the egg yolks, whole egg, Pecorino, Parmigiano, and most of the black pepper in a bowl.
  4. Toss the spaghetti with the guanciale and a spoon of fat off the heat. Add the egg mixture, loosening with pasta water until glossy.
  5. Finish with more Pecorino and black pepper.
