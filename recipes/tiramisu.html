Tiramisu

Mascarpone, coffee, restraint.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • 4 egg yolks
  • 80 g sugar
  • 500 g mascarpone
  • 250 ml espresso, cooled
  • 2 tbsp marsala
  • 24 savoiardi
  • Cocoa powder

Instructions

  1. Whisk the egg yolks and sugar to a thick ribbon over gentle heat; cool.
  2. Fold in the mascarpone until smooth.
  3. Dip the savoiardi briefly in the espresso and marsala.
  4. Layer the biscuits and mascarpone cream; chill well. Dust with cocoa before serving.
