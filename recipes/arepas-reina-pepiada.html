Arepas Reina Pepiada

Corn cakes with avocado-chicken filling.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • Masarepa
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Cooked chicken
  • Avocado
  • Mayonnaise
  • Lime
  • Cilantro

Instructions

  1. Make a soft dough from masarepa, water, and salt; shape and griddle until crusted.
  2. Finish the arepas in the oven if needed.
  3. Mix the shredded chicken with avocado, mayonnaise, lime, and cilantro.
  4. Split and fill.
