French Onion Soup

Onions cooked all the way through.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • 1 kg onions, thinly sliced
  • 50 g butter
  • 1 tbsp flour
  • 150 ml dry white wine
  • 1.2 L stock
  • Baguette slices
  • Gruyère

Instructions

  1. Cook onions slowly in butter until deeply brown and sweet.
  2. Add flour and cook briefly. Deglaze with wine, then add stock.
  3. Simmer until integrated and rich.
  4. Top with toasted bread and Gruyère; gratin until molten.
