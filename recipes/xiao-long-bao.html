Xiao Long Bao

Soup dumplings, no shortcuts.

IngredientsInstructions

Ingredients

  • Dumpling wrappers
  • Ground pork
  • Ginger
  • Scallions
  • Soy sauce
  • Shaoxing wine
  • Aspic

Instructions

  1. Mix filling with ginger, scallions, soy, wine, and diced aspic.
  2. Wrap tightly with pleats.
  3. Steam until the wrappers are translucent and the soup has melted inside.
